Battle a pokemon master in one of the 3 difficulty levels. At the start of the game you and your opponent will recieve random pokemons with random moves (each pokemon will only have moves that would be possible for that pokemon to learn).

There are no levels in the pokemons that each of you recieve, just the base stats of each one and types. Types, power, and stats are used to calculate the damage on each move used.

The first trainer to run out of pokemons lose! If you are good enough to win then you will become the new Pokemon Master!
